This worth-lawsuit dish takes less than 45 minutes to make and yield 2 burgers.
Probably cost more than going to mcDonalds but at least you know what you are eating.

Ingredients

  • ⅔ pound ground beef (85% lean)
  • 2 cups mayonnaise
  • 2 tablespoons prepared yellow mustard
  • 2 tablespoons sweet pickle relish
  • ½ teaspoons white wine vinegar, or more to taste
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon ground paprika
  • 4 hamburger bun bottoms
  • 2 hamburger bun tops
  • 2 tablespoons minced onion, or to taste
  • ½ cup shredded lettuce, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons dill pickle slices, or to taste
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Nutrition

126 calories; protein 11.1g; carbohydrates 0.7g; fat 8.8g; cholesterol 327.4mg; sodium 162mg.


Steps bros

  1. Spray a large glass bowl with cooking spray.

  2. Crack 1 egg into a small ramekin and then pour into the bowl, whisk egg.

  3. Repeat with remaining eggs, whisking well after each addition.

  4. Cook eggs in a microwave oven for 30 seconds; remove from oven and stir.

  5. Continue cooking in 30-second intervals, stirring after each, until eggs are almost set, about 3 minutes total.

  6. Season with salt and pepper.
